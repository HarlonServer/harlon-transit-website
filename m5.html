Meet your new connection to everything Harlon

Say hello to Harlon's most advanced metro line yet, with great connections citywide, plus new tech and features that make it as easy as ABC to get around your city of Harlon.

All About Line M5

The Triangle

The northern terminus of the line. Change for Metro Line M1 and M2 services.

Sergels Square

Entertainment Shopping

Alight here for the Sergels Square Arcade and Mustin Granger Sergels Mall. Change here for Metro Line M3 and Commuter services, as well as Mainline services operated by HN in the future.

Folyopart Road

Alight here for ferry services from Folyopart Pier, a short walk aboveground from the entrance of the station. The Great Market Hall is also a few blocks away from the station.

Oakmont Plaza

Alight here for Harlonian institutions such as the Harlonian Stock Exchange, The Crystal Club, and Kovacs Library.

Southbank Circus

Alight here for City Hall, a short walk away from the station entrance. Change here for Metro Line M2 services.

Times Square

Shopping

With a station entrance that surfaces right in the heart of it all, SouMa Shopping Mall, streetside shops and banks, and Stockholm Park are just a stone's throw from the station entrance.

Johnson Park

Hockey

Alight here for the Johnson Park Hockey Stadium, where you can watch hockey games and cheer on your favourite team on game day. You can even have a go at a game of your own on the rink when the stadium isn't being used for games.

Seaporte Junction

Change here for Commuter services as well as Mainline services in the future. This is one of the two aboveground stations on the line, the other being Seaporte Docks.

Seaporte Docks

Food Entertainment

The southern terminus of the line, and the other aboveground M5 station. The shipping and logistics hub of Seaporte and ferry and cruise ship terminals are on the doorstep of this station, as are a medical centre, food court and a rooftop club.

M5 Highlights

Take a look at the new features and tech that we've introduced to make your experience even better.

Platform Screen Doors

Automated doors protect you from the platform edge and open and close with the train doors.

Station Information

Passenger Information Displays at stations guide you to the correct platform and inform you about the approaching train.

Onboard Information

New Onboard Passenger Information Displays and audio announcements allow you to easily know where you are on the line.

Priority Seating and Accessible Spaces

Specially marked spaces onboard our X Stock trains allow us to accomodate those who need a seat, wheelchair users and prams.
