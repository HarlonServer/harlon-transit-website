Welcome Back, M31!

Metro Line M31 has now reopened, with new trains, tracks, signs, signals, and an extension to Roslagen via Upminster, plus improved stations and scenery. There's more to see and more to do on Line M31!

On this page
  • Meet the new M31
  • Employment Opportunities

Meet the new M31

Duluth

Depot

Change for Metro Line M3 services. Head to the depot here to start your career as a train driver!

Shrewsberry

Signal Box

Alight here for the Signal Box, where you can train as a signaller to work the signals on the line.

Long Island Airfield

Alight for Long Island Airfield.

Downminster

Change for ferry services.

Upminster Bridge

A single track heritage listed swing bridge that carries the line between Downminster and Upminster. You might even spot a Harlonian resident rowing their boat on the water!

Upminster

Change for Commuter Line C11 and ferry services.

Roslagen

Alight for Roslagen Tree Plantation, where you can also connect to Bus Route 11, which travels north to Stenford Bay and south in the other direction to Wixley Beach.

Major interchanges along Bus Route 11 include Wixley Beach, where you can change for Metro Line M11 and ferry services, Oxville, where you can change for Commuter Line C1 services, and Stenford Seafront, where you can connect to various independent Stenford Bay railways and Commuter Line C1 services on the other side of Stenford Bay.

Employment Opportunities

A refurbished line brings employment opportunities. Start your career as a train driver or signaller today!

Role Salary (£) Salary Unit Training

M31 Train Driver

£50

Per every completed end-to-end run, following speed limits and signals and stopping at all stations. Attempts to cheat the system will result in a ban.

Proceed to Duluth Depot by taking Metro Line M3 and read through the driver training manual to become a train driver.

M31 Signaller

£30

Every ten (10) minutes of signalling work

Open a ticket in the #help channel on the HCS Discord and request a signaller training session. Please specify your timezone and availabilities in UTC/GMT.
