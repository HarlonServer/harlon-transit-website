Your new connection to everything Harlon

HarlonTransit is excited to announce that Metro Line M5 is finally open. Connecting The Triangle with Seaporte Docks, M5 gets you to familiar major interchange stations but also major destinations in the City of Harlon, such as Johnson Park, Times Square and the Great Market Hall.
